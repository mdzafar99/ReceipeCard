Chocolate Cake

Preparation Time: 45 mins | Serving Size: 6

Ingredients

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1/2 cup cocoa powder
  • 2 eggs
  • 1/2 cup butter
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 1/2 cup milk

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 180¬∞C.
  2. Mix flour, sugar, and cocoa powder.
  3. Add eggs, butter, and milk; whisk until smooth.
  4. Pour the batter into a greased baking pan.
  5. Bake for 30‚Äì35 minutes.
  6. Cool and serve with chocolate glaze.
